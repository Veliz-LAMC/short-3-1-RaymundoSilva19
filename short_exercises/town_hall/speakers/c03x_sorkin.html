Andrew Ross Sorkin

Andrew Ross Sorkin is a prominent journalist, author, and financial columnist. He is best known as the co-founder of the influential financial news website DealBook, which is published by The New York Times. Sorkin is also a regular contributor to CNBC's Squawk Box and is the anchor of the network's prime-time program Squawk Box.
